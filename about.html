About

Spitshine VFX is a team of VFX artists skilled in video cleanup, retouching, and other visual effects services for commercials, film, TV, music videos, reels, and more. Based in Metro Detroit, the company is fully remote and has served clients well beyond Southeast Michigan: from Chicago, to New York City, to Atlanta, to Los Angeles. 
‍
Origin: After providing a decade of corporate visual effects support, founder Grant Pichla launched Spitshine VFX in 2023 to bring his team's VFX services to agencies, in-house corporate teams, production companies, filmmakers, music artists, and more.
